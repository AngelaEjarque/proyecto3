RULES

The mastermind game consist in guess the right combination of colors including the right position, before the rounds end.
The same color can be in more than one celd, be careful!.
The player has to enter the combination he thinks is correct.
To do that, the player has to click on the colour and click on the celd from bottom to top in every row depending on what round is.
Then, press the "check" button.
There are some circles at the right of the screen, that represents if you're right or not.

Purple means is the right color in right position.
White means right color in wrong position.
If there is no color, means the color is not in the winning combination.
