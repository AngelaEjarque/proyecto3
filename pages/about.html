RULES

The mastermind game consist in guess the right combination of colors including the right position, before the rounds end. The player has to enter the combination he thinks is correct. To do that, the player has to click on the colour and click on the celd from bottom to top in every row depending on what round is. Then, press the "check" button. There are some circles at the right of the screen, that represents if you're right or not, showing purple if it's right or white if it's wrong.
